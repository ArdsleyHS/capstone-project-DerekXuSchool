Other Useful Websites

  • Art of Problem Solving

    This website has an amazing amount of resources; it has the problems and solutions of almost all the most important math competitions in the world, a bunch of courses and books that are extremely good for getting into competition math, and even forums where you can ask questions and read posts by other people interested in math.

  • Omegalearn

    This website has a comprehensive list of the most important math competitions in the US and an ebook that includes nearly all the formulas/theorems you'll need for pre-olympiad math competitions. It also has a lot of information on how to prepare for pre-olympiad math competitions.

  • Evan Chen's Blog

    This website has lots of information for people who are interested in the higher levels of competition math: the olympiads. It also has a lot of information about how to get started/proceed in your math competition journey.
