About Me

I'm Derek Xu, a freshman at Ardsley High School. My hobbies include playing the violin, lifting weights, and studying math. Out of these, I've probably devoted the most time to math; I've been studying it outside of school for over 10 years, and I've recently grown to love doing so, especially in preparation for competitions. Although my competition experience hasn't been exceedingly extensive, I hope to expand my horizons and both participate in more competitions and perform better in the ones that I participate in. This website showcases a few theorems that I found especially interesting or useful when learning them. Enjoy!
